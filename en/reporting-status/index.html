Reporting Status

The reporting status provides an overview of how many SDG indicators are reported on this platform.
If information is available for an indicator, it is marked as Reported . For other indicators, a suitable data source is still being sought; these can be identified by the Not available mark. If the time series for an indicator are currently being prepared for presentation on this platform, these indicators are marked In progress .
Time series for Germany are not available for all of the globally agreed indicators. There may be various reasons for this: Certain internationally relevant indicators cannot be meaningfully calculated for Germany or can only be answered in a global context. Such indicators are marked as Not applicable .
